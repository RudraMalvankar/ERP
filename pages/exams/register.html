Terms and Conditions - Exam Registration

Examination Registration Terms

  1. Registration Deadline: All registrations must be completed before the specified deadline. Late registrations will not be accepted.
  2. Fee Payment: Examination fees are non-refundable once paid. Fees must be paid within 24 hours of registration completion.
  3. Subject Selection: Students can only register for subjects they are enrolled in for the current semester.
  4. Identity Verification: Students must carry valid photo identification during examinations.
  5. Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance is required to be eligible for examinations.
  6. Hall Ticket: Hall tickets will be available for download 7 days before the examination date.
  7. Exam Rules: Students must follow all examination rules and regulations. Violation may result in disqualification.
  8. Results: Results will be declared within 30 days of the last examination date.
  9. Re-evaluation: Applications for re-evaluation must be submitted within 15 days of result declaration.
  10. Data Privacy: Personal data will be used only for examination purposes and will be handled as per privacy policy.
